passman - local password manager

passman is a lightweight, local password manager built in ruby. it is designed to securely store your passwords using aes-256 encryption, ensuring your sensitive information stays safe and private. unlike cloud-based password managers, passman keeps all data stored locally on your machine, giving you full control over your passwords.

features:

  • local storage: all passwords are stored locally in an encrypted file.
  • encryption: aes-256 encryption ensures the security of your passwords.
  • no cloud: data is never uploaded to any server, keeping everything on your local machine.
  • password management: add, edit, and delete passwords with ease.
  • password generation: generate strong passwords to enhance security.
  • secure retrieval: access your stored passwords with a master password.

passman is ideal for anyone who wants a simple and secure password manager without relying on cloud services. it's designed for privacy-conscious users who prefer a lightweight solution that can be fully controlled locally.
